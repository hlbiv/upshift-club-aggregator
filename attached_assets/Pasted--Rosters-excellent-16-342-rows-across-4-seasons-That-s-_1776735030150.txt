## What went wrong

`extract_head_coach_from_html` — the inline extractor I hooked into the bulk loop in PR-7 — was designed against the UCLA test fixture where the roster page happens to include a `.sidearm-staff-member` block inline. In practice, **most D1 roster pages don't include staff inline** — they link out to a separate `/sports/mens-soccer/coaches` or `/sports/mens-soccer/staff-directory` page.

The 1 coach we got is probably UCLA or a similar program whose roster page actually does ship staff inline. The other ~149 D1 programs keep staff on a sibling URL.

## Fix path (small, follow-up PR)

Same pattern as PR-2's `ncaa-resolve-urls`: add a new step that probes `/coaches` / `/staff-directory` URLs per program and scrapes staff from the dedicated page. Reuse the HEAD-probe resolver from PR-3.

**Proposed shape — PR-9 "ncaa-coaches":**
1. New source key `--source ncaa-coaches` (maybe combined with `--all --division --gender --backfill-seasons N` to mirror the roster flow)

   - `/sports/<sport>/coaches`

3. Parse the staff page; extract all coaches (not just head) — same multi-strategy pattern as `parse_roster_html`
4. Write each coach's `(college_id, name, title, academic_year)` tenure row + update `college_coaches`
