## One thing worth pushing on

**Step 4's "force to academy" override is underspecified and worth tightening before execution.** The plan says "any club whose *top* affiliation has `league_family` matching the MLS Academy family is forced to academy." That's ambiguous in one case:

- Concorde Fire has ECNL Boys (elite) and, let's say hypothetically, a single MLS NEXT team.
- Under "top affiliation = MLS Academy" logic, Concorde Fire would become `academy`.
- But Concorde Fire isn't an MLS Academy club — they just field one team in that league (hypothetically).

Real MLS Academy clubs (FC Dallas, LAFC, Inter Miami, the 29 scraped in March) are **pro-pathway clubs whose entire identity is the academy.** A competitive club fielding one MLS NEXT team is different.

The safer override rule:

> Force `academy` only if the club's **primary** affiliation (highest `tier_numeric`) is in the MLS Academy family, AND the club name / metadata signals it's an academy club, not a competitive club with one academy team.

In practice this probably doesn't trigger for real data — the 29 MLS Academy clubs almost certainly don't also play in ECNL — but the rule as written could misclassify edge cases. Worth a one-line amendment.
